Web 3.0 Workshop

Date: March 12, 2023

We are thrilled to share that the GDSC NMIT club has been organizing a series of successful sessions on emerging technological trends, providing our members with incredible learning opportunities. This workshop featured four outstanding speakers and had over 50 active participants. The hands-on session provided an excellent introduction to web3 and insightful discussions on cryptocurrency using MetaMask and the highly scalable, smart contract platform, Shardeum.

We want to express our sincere appreciation to our enthusiastic participants, speakers, faculty coordinators, and the entire team who made this event possible. GDSC NMIT is very grateful to Dr. Mohan S Ph.D, Hod ISE Dept, NMIT and Mr. Mohan Kumar T G, the faculty coordinator for their immense support in bringing this opportunity for the students.

The success of this workshop is a testament to our commitment to providing our community with the latest and most innovative learning opportunities.

Inauguration of GDSC NMIT

Date: [Insert Date]

The Google Developer Student Club at Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, initiated by the Department of Information Science and Engineering, recently hosted the inauguration of our club, and it was a huge success! We were honored to have Kishore Jagannath, a Cloud Engineer at Google, and Aanshul Sadaria, a Software Engineer 3 at Google, join us as guest speakers. Both shared their personal journeys and experiences, providing valuable insight and inspiration to our fellow students. Kishore Jagannath also gave a comprehensive overview of the Google Cloud Platform, which generated a lot of interest among the attendees. NITTE Administrator Shri. Rohit Punja addressed all the students with an insightful story.

We are grateful for the support and guidance of Dr. Mohan S Ph.D, Professor and Head of Department ISE and GDSC Faculty Advisor Mr. Mohan Kumar T G.

We look forward to future opportunities to collaborate and learn from industry leaders.
